Source catalogues

Object attributes and orbits are drawn from two catalogues. Both are freely available from their providers under their respective terms; obtain the raw data directly from the sources.

SourceContribution to the modelAccess
ESA DISCOSObject physical attributes: mass, cross-section, class, dimensions, predicted decay.discosweb.esoc.esa.int
US Space-Track (18 SDS)Orbital elements: apogee, perigee, inclination, period, RCS size, object type.space-track.org
Space-Track data is provided under the Space-Track user agreement, which restricts redistribution of the raw catalogue. Aggregate and derived products published on this page (e.g. shell densities) are the work of this project; per-object catalogue data should be obtained directly from Space-Track and DISCOS under their terms.
